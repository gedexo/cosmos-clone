About Us

Founded in 1990, Cosmos sports has come a long way from it's humble beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for helping others to develop life skills through sports, providing the best equipment's for a healthy life style drove us to do intense research and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming brand. Sports help children learn lifelong skills, empower women, engage men, and remove descrimination in communities. Sports provide a platform for people to come together to build and sustain values.

We now serve customers all over India and Middle East and are thrilled to be a part of fair-trade wing of the real industry. In wholesale and licensing, the group retains a portfolio of world-famous brands as well as our own brands. In addition to this Cosmos sports is actively planning to expand with an aim to make their sports and inclusive for all by supporting the community commitment to a healthy lifestyle.

Sports Infrastructure Development

Cosmos Sports has state-of-the-art technology and renowned experts to produce the best of the sports infrastructure, specialising in synthetic sports surfaces, running tracks and indoor sports wooden courts. We are also specialised in building stadiums.

Cosmos Sports City Sharjah

The Cosmos Sports City in Al Sharjah has been designed with the mission of creating a challenging sport academic environment while incorporating a modern athletic programme that can be enjoyed by all. The sports club in Cosmos Sports City caters a diverse range of sports training and coaching by highly experienced and international level coaches. Its various sports courts with world-class facilities include - Cricket, Volleyball, MMA/Kick Boxing Ring, Table Tennis, Wall Climbing, Basket Ball, Badminton, Football, Skating, Paddle Court, Male & Female Gym, Swimming Pool, Gymnastic, and Table Tennis. The Cosmos Sports City, Al Sharjah also features Ayurveda & Holistic Spa, Health Bar, Retail Store and Exhibition Hall which makes it a one-stop shop for all sports-related requirements.

Cosmos Sports City Al Karama

With top-class facilities and guidance from expert coaches, Cosmos Badminton Academy at Al Karama, Dubai, oers the best badminton training to individuals of any age and gender. The academy is looking forward to hosting various camps and activities in the future.
